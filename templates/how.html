How does it work?

SwappingPlace, the platform dedicated to clothing donations and exchanges among users. SwappingPlace provides an innovative alternative for refreshing your wardrobe without resorting to traditional purchases. Discover clothing that finds a new life with a straightforward process: reserve an item online and pick it up from the donor.

Donate

To become a Donor, you first need to create an account. Then create your online store. All you have to do is enter a postal address and add clothes. You wait for a collector to reserve one of your garments, then you agree on a date for them to pick it up. That's all there is to it.

Collector

To become a Collector, you just need to create an account. Look for a garment you like, reserve it and agree with the donor on a date to pick it up.
